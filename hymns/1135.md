# 1135 - Bless God!

## Notation

Eb - 4/4 on 4 - SOL

## Lyrics

### Verse 1

Bless God for all He's done! Bless God for Christ, His Son! O, let us magnify Him for He's holy, holy! One voice in unity, One voice of praise to Thee, With hearts of love and worship we will sing, " Bless God!"
